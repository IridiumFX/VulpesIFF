VPS_StreamReader	reads from socket/file and uses a Decoder to produce a VPS_Data
					of the raw bytes
VPS_DataReader 		Wraps the VPS_Data and is used to access the data from these raw bytes
IFF_Reader			Wraps on the VPS_StreamReader and VPS_DataReader.
					It uses the StreamReader to procure the data then the DataReader
					to access it. Provides the primitives to read a size, a tag, a chunk
